# Quiz instructions

Generate 2 or 3 end-of-chapter quiz questions for the Active Chapter.
Constraints:

- Test understanding, not recall. Prefer "why does X happen" or "what would
  you change to do Y" over "what is the definition of Z".
- Each question must be answerable from material in the Active Chapter (and
  earlier chapters the student has already read). Do not ask about topics
  introduced later in the book.
- Phrase as plain questions, one per item. Do not include answers, numbering,
  or explanations.

Emit the result through the provided tool as a list of question strings.
